55.4 List operations

— Function: stack-find proc stk
— Function: stack-for-all proc stk
— Function: stack-exists proc stk

Apply find or for-all or exists to the internal list of elements, return the result.

— Method on <stack>: find proc
— Method on <stack>: for-all proc
— Method on <stack>: exists proc

Apply find or for-all or exists to the internal list of elements, return the result.

— Function: stack-remp! proc stk
— Function: stack-remove! obj stk
— Function: stack-remv! obj stk
— Function: stack-remq! obj stk

Apply remp or remove or remv or remq to the internal list of elements, removing objects from the stack.

— Method on <stack>: remp! proc
— Method on <stack>: remove! obj
— Method on <stack>: remv! obj
— Method on <stack>: remq! obj

Apply remp or remove or remv or remq to the internal list of elements, removing objects from the stack.

— Function: stack-memp proc stk
— Function: stack-member obj stk
— Function: stack-memv obj stk
— Function: stack-memq obj stk

Apply memp or member or memv or memq to the internal list of elements, return the result.

— Method on <stack>: memp proc
— Method on <stack>: member obj
— Method on <stack>: memv obj
— Method on <stack>: memq obj

Apply memp or member or memv or memq to the internal list of elements, return the result.
