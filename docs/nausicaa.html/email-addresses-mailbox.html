60.1.2.5 Mailbox
— Class: <mailbox>

Hold a mailbox specification.

— Immutable Field of <mailbox>: display-name

Holds the phrase string.

— Immutable Field of <mailbox>: route

Holds #f or a <route> record.

— Immutable Field of <mailbox>: addr-spec

Holds an <addr-spec> record.

— Function: <mailbox>-display-name mb
— Function: <mailbox>-route mb
— Function: <mailbox>-addr-spec mb

Field accessors.

— Generic Function: object->string (o <mailbox>)

Return the string representation of o.
