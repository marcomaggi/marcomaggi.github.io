75.1.2 Computing the check digit

The following bindings are exported by the (nausicaa standard-numbers luhn) library.

— Function: sequence->luhn10-check-digit getter

Compute and return the check digit for a sequence of fixnums in the range [0, 9]. getter must be a thunk returning the sequence of fixnums one at a time and returning #f when no more fixnums are available. 

          #!r6rs
          (import (nausicaa)
            (nausicaa standard-numbers luhn))
          
          (define (make-list-getter sequence)
            (lambda ()
              (and (not (null? sequence))
                   (begin0
                       (car sequence)
                     (set! sequence (cdr sequence))))))
          
          (sequence->luhn10-check-digit
            (make-list-getter '(8 2 3 4 5)))
          ⇒ 0
          
          (sequence->luhn10-check-digit
            (make-list-getter (reverse '(4 9 9 2 7 3 9 8 7 1))))
          ⇒ 6
          
          (sequence->luhn10-check-digit
            (make-list-getter (reverse '(3 0 2))))
          ⇒ 0
          
          (sequence->luhn10-check-digit
            (make-list-getter (reverse '(3 1 2))))
          ⇒ 9
— Function: number->luhn10-check-digit integer

Compute and return the check digit for the exact integer number integer. 

          (number->luhn10-check-digit 54328)      ⇒ 0
          (number->luhn10-check-digit 4992739871) ⇒ 6
          (number->luhn10-check-digit 302)        ⇒ 0
          (number->luhn10-check-digit 312)        ⇒ 9
— Function: list->luhn10-check-digit ell

Given a list ell of fixnums in the range [0, 9], compute and return the check digit; the list is interpreted as a sequence of digits starting from its car. No validation is performed on the input list. 

          (list->luhn10-check-digit '(2 0 3)) ⇒ 0
          (list->luhn10-check-digit '(2 1 3)) ⇒ 9
— Function: string->luhn10-check-digit str

Given a string str of digits in the range [0, 9], compute and return the check digit; the string is interpreted as sequence of digits starting from the rightmost character. No validation is performed on the input string. 

          (string->luhn10-check-digit "54328")        ⇒ 0
          (string->luhn10-check-digit "4992739871")   ⇒ 6
          (string->luhn10-check-digit "302")          ⇒ 0
          (string->luhn10-check-digit "312")          ⇒ 9
— Function: vector->left-luhn10-check-digit v
— Function: vector->right-luhn10-check-digit v

Given a vector v of fixnums in the range [0, 9], compute and return the check digit. No validation is performed on the input vector.

vector->left-luhn10-check-digit considers v as a sequence of digits starting from the leftmost element.

vector->right-luhn10-check-digit considers v as a sequence of digits starting from the rightmost element. 

          (vector->left-luhn10-check-digit '#(8 2 3 4 5))
          ⇒ 0
          
          (vector->left-luhn10-check-digit '#(1 7 8 9 3 7 2 9 9 4))
          ⇒ 6
          
          (vector->left-luhn10-check-digit '#(2 0 3))
          ⇒ 0
          
          (vector->left-luhn10-check-digit '#(2 1 3))
          ⇒ 9
          
          (vector->right-luhn10-check-digit '#(5 4 3 2 8))
          ⇒ 0
          
          (vector->right-luhn10-check-digit '#(4 9 9 2 7 3 9 8 7 1))
          ⇒ 6
          
          (vector->right-luhn10-check-digit '#(3 0 2))
          ⇒ 0
          
          (vector->right-luhn10-check-digit '#(3 1 2))
          ⇒ 9
— Function: bytevector->left-luhn10-check-digit bv
— Function: bytevector->right-luhn10-check-digit bv

Given a bytevector bv of bytes in the range [0, 9], compute and return the check digit. No validation is performed on the input bytevector.

bytevector->left-luhn10-check-digit considers bv as a sequence of digits starting from the leftmost byte.

bytevector->right-luhn10-check-digit considers bv as a sequence of digits starting from the rightmost byte. 

          (bytevector->left-luhn10-check-digit '#vu8(8 2 3 4 5))
          ⇒ 0
          
          (bytevector->left-luhn10-check-digit '#vu8(1 7 8 9 3 7 2 9 9 4))
          ⇒ 6
          
          (bytevector->left-luhn10-check-digit '#vu8(2 0 3))
          ⇒ 0
          
          (bytevector->left-luhn10-check-digit '#vu8(2 1 3))
          ⇒ 9
          
          (bytevector->right-luhn10-check-digit '#vu8(5 4 3 2 8))
          ⇒ 0
          
          (bytevector->right-luhn10-check-digit '#vu8(4 9 9 2 7 3 9 8 7 1))
          ⇒ 6
          
          (bytevector->right-luhn10-check-digit '#vu8(3 0 2))
          ⇒ 0
          
          (bytevector->right-luhn10-check-digit '#vu8(3 1 2))
          ⇒ 9
