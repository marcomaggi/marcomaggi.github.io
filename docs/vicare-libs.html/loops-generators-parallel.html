1.20.4.8 Parallel generators
— Generator Syntax: :parallel ?generator ...

Run several generators in parallel. This means that the next binding in the sequence is obtained by advancing each generator by one step. The parallel generator terminates when any of its component generators terminate. The generators share a common scope for the variables they introduce. This implies that the names of the variables introduced by the various generators must be distinct.
