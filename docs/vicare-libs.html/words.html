3.5 Exact integer predicates and constants

It is often needed to validate exact integers agains fixed range bounds, for example for 32-bit and 64-bit representations or some platform specific C language type. The library (vicare platform words) implements such predicates and minimum/maximum range constants.

  • words syntaxes: Miscellaneous syntaxes.
  • words sizeof: Knowing the size of C language types.
  • words predicates: Verifying the range of integers.
  • words limits: Exact integer range limits.
