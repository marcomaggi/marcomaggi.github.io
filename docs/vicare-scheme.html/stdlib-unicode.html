5.1 Unicode

The procedures exported by the (rnrs unicode (6)) library provide access to some aspects of the Unicode semantics for characters and strings: category information, case–independent comparisons, case mappings, and normalization.

Some of the procedures that operate on characters or strings ignore the difference between upper case and lower case. These procedures have -ci (for “case insensitive”) embedded in their names.
