I will take a break from programming in the Scheme language, again; nobody will care, but me; that is fine ¶

Posted on Oct 3, 2019

This year, during spring and summer, I ported some of my old Scheme code to CHICKEN. For awhile it has been fun. Some days I even coded into late nights, because it was just pure fun I had not experienced in quite some time.

From my point of view: there is a lot to do to bring the CHICKEN programming environment up to the level of usability and features that I would like. Too much work. I do not feel like doing again what I did with Vicare Scheme. I do not have that energy in my mind.

So, break it is. I will go back to some of my C language projects. I’ve been feeling depleted lately. Life is hard…
