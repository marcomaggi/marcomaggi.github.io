Marco’s Weblog ¶

Ciao, I am Marco Maggi, one of the hundreds with this name in Italy; there are more in the rest of the world. This is my weblog; it started mostly about programming in the Scheme language and developing Vicare in particular, but now it is about everything. It is written using gnu Texinfo.

I am on GitHub, on Bitbucket, I have a Google account with a YouTube page for my music playlists; I also have this weblog. And that’s it. No Facebook, no Instagram, no Twitter, no whatever else. I do not chat, so I am not on any chat channels.

No comments on this weblog.

If you want to give me a piece of your mind about general topics: take your chances by emailing me at mrc.mgg@gmail.com. Be polite, thank you.
