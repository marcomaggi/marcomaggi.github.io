Video of the day

Posted on Sun Apr 30, 2017

The Queen are officially my favourite band. Watching the video below on television, I want it all, convinced me to buy the album’s audio cassette: the first album I bought in my life. I knew only this song, all the others were totally new to me when I first listened them at home; I was shocked by the two opening songs, which sounded really weird to my untrained ears; but then I was not disappointed, this album opened new musical horizons to me.
