Posted on Mar 2, 2025

What an ugliness! If I start ranting about politics, it can only go downhill…

I wonder if Italy is on the verge of another petty crisis. In the early 1990s there was no more tax money to circle around to pay bribes to politicians: a bit of shaking ensued, with poor, unfortunate, politicians retiring in famous tourist places so that they could whine until the end of their days, while sipping drinks and bathing in the sun.

Today we are running out of tax money again: there is no more money to condone tax evasion and elusion. Governmental institutions, bot local and national, might fall because of this. Ha! Ha! Ha! Poor politicians…

Being so nihilist is not my standard, but sheesh…

We need money so that we can buy tactical nuclear bombs from France. Double “Ha! Ha! Ha!”
