Stuff on life ¶

Posted on Jan 26, 2025

For once I will be serious; without drama; without comedy. I’m not obese, according to the definition; I have surpassed the threshold of 102 centimetres at the waist, which makes visceral fat a thing. It is not because I have become more muscular.

I know what I have to do; doing it is another thing; I have to admit that: if I had someone to report to, it would be easier; but I report only to myself. And that is life.
