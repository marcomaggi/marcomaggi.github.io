Stuff on world ¶

Posted on May 24, 2024

This is not a thematic weblog, it is my weblog; I do not want to make it a political weblog; I also do not want to live in the magical world of Fantasystan, where nothing touches me.

Death is feasting in Palestine; there’s been a war going on since the creation of Israel; a single endless war; some people were killed even before World War II; war is a series of atrocities; there’s been many of them.

I generally despise Muslim culture, but I cannot ignore that every report I have read states that: from the beginning of this single war, many more Palestinians were killed than Israelis were killed.

There are useless debates about who is Right and who is Wrong; after all the atrocities: nobody can be Right, everybody is Wrong.

Both parties’ cultures have deep roots in religion; you cannot reason with people like that; if a solution is possible, it will not come from them.

People from outside also want to adopt some dogma: they pick a side and turn off morality and critical thinking.

Is there anyone that actually wants to stop war?
