Stuff on life ¶

Posted on May 24, 2024

One of the few advantages of being single is that: if I have the opportunity to go someplace, I can just go; no planning, no negotiating; I put on my shoes and I’m out. I still have obligations, so I cannot go far and I cannot stay for long; just seeing something different feels good. I have rarely taken advantage of this, which is stupid; I’m trying to change as part of the process of dragging myself out of a slump.
