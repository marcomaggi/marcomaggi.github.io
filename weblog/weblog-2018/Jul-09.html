Documentation writer’s block

Posted on Jul 9, 2018

In the latest months I’m really struggling with it. If I have an idea for some code to write: I’m at it immediately. But when I have to document the new code or update the documentation after a change: I’m stuck. I open the Emacs session, I stare at the files… then read a newspaper on the Net… or a science fiction site, or a comics site… and I’m gone with the wind.

It was not like this just a couple of years ago. But now it is. Am I loosing it?

I have two projects stuck like this. It sucks. I have already verified that eating a lot of ice cream does not fix it.
