Goodbye Sourceforge, Hello Sourceforge!

Posted on Oct 26, 2018

Yesterday I made a deep cleanup of my Net accounts; changed some passwords; reorganised the paper copy of the accounts informations. I really needed it. There was some old stuff that no longer exists, like an Infogami account (rip).

Among the oldest accounts, there is a Sourceforge one. I actually created two accounts, a very old one in 2003 (of which I lost the credentials) and a new one in 2013. Sourceforge always had an uglish interface in my eyes: the webpages do not guide my eyes to the important informations. This is still so.

One of the things that troubled me was the impossibility of removing one’s own projects. Well, it appears that now this is solved: yesterday I was able to delete all my projects under the newest account. It is now clean!

So now, I am considering to restart using it for something. As a backup of some stuff I keep at Github. It would be perfect if I could join the two accounts into a single one…
